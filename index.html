Master of Research in Political Science, Sciences Po Paris
My research examines how identity anxiety, perceived exclusion, and ontological insecurity shape political radicalization, collective alignment, and state-society relations. I use causal inference, survey and experimental methods, and visual analysis to understand psychological and institutional responses to crisis.
