Research

My research explores how identity anxiety, perceived exclusion, and ontological insecurity shape political radicalization and collective alignment. I draw on theories from political psychology, public opinion, and authoritarian governance to analyze how crises and symbolic threats influence political behavior across individual, national, and international levels.
